GST Registration Details

Why register for GST? Mandatory for businesses with turnover above ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for services).

Benefits:

  • ✓ Legally recognized as supplier of goods/services
  • ✓ Input tax credit benefits
  • ✓ Competitive advantage in business

Required Documents:

  • PAN of business
  • Aadhaar of proprietor/partners
  • Business address proof
  • Bank account details

Process:

  1. Online application on GST portal
  2. Upload documents
  3. Verification by authorities
  4. GSTIN issued within 3–7 days

Income Tax Return Filing Details

Why file ITR? Mandatory if income exceeds ₹2.5 lakhs or for claiming refunds.

Benefits:

  • ✓ Avoid penalties
  • ✓ Easy loan and visa approvals
  • ✓ Claim refunds and losses

Required Documents:

  • PAN and Aadhaar
  • Form 16 / Salary slips
  • Bank account details
  • Investment proofs for deductions

Process:

  1. Collect income and tax details
  2. Prepare computation
  3. Upload on Income Tax portal
  4. Verify via Aadhaar or Net Banking
